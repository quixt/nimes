Other Attractions in Nîmes

Les Halles de Nîmes

A farmers market selling local produces. You won't be able to resist the taste of oysters and fish caught by local fishermen or the picholine olives. If you are hungry for lunch there are many food bars selling a variety of local dishes.

Costières de Nîmes

Sourcing fresh grapes from the Rhône Valley, the Costières de Nîmes wines are hard to beat. With many stunningly delicious wines made by some of the finest winemakers around, this is an important stop for wine connoisseurs.

Jardin de La Fontaine

The Jardins de la Fontaine is one of the most beautiful gardens in all of France. The garden is full of thriving flowers and plants, and the stone fountain at its center is truly a sight to behold. Visitors to the garden can enjoy the peaceful atmosphere and take in the stunning natural beauty.
