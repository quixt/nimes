Events in Nîmes

Festival de Nîmes

The historic city of Nimes in the south of France is the perfect setting for the Festival de Nimes. This annual music festival takes place in the beautiful Roman amphitheatre. The festival features a wide range of music, from classical and opera to pop and rock. There is something for everyone.

June 17 - July 20 on varying days

Pentecost Feria

The annual Pentecost Feria bullfights in Nimes, France are a popular tourist attraction. Every year, bullfighters from all over the world come to Nimes to compete in the feria. The bullfights are held in an arena in the center of the city, and they are always well-attended.

June 5

The Great Games of Rome

If you are a fan of ancient history, then the Games of Rome reenactments in Nimes, France are definitely something you will want to check out. Every year, people from all over the world come to Nimes to take part in these games, which are a recreation of the ancient Olympic Games.

October 7 - 11
