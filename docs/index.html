Nîmes, France

Nîmes is a beautiful, historic city in the South of France. Dating back to the Roman Empire, Nîmes is filled with ancient Roman ruins. Nîmes is also known for its famous museums and its unique architecture. Nîmes is a city of art, culture and history, but is also known for its wines and nightlife
