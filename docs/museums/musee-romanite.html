Musée de la Romanité

❮ ❯

The Musée de la Romanité is a newly opened modern museum showcasing old Roman artifacts. The museum is located in the heart of Nîmes, just a short walk from the city's main square. Visitors can explore roman villa recreations, medieval art and artifacts, roman sculptures, and much more.

The Musée de la Romanité is a must-see for anyone visiting Nîmes. It is interactive and easily accessible, and highly interesting. Visitors not into history may also enjoy the garden.
